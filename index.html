The Princess Matching Game!

HOW TO PLAY:
  • Click on a card to show what Princess is hiding underneath
  • Remember where the Princess is hiding!
  • Click another card to see if you can find the matching Princess
  • Each time the cards do not match, you lose a point from the Score above
  • If you choose a matching pair, your points do not drop!
  • Find all the matching pairs before your points run out
Ready? Let's go!
